DEN'S PENS

ABOUT

My name is Dennis Humm. I'm a father to two beautiful girls and husband to one kick-ass roller derby wife. I'm in my mid-to-late 30's and am an avid collector of fountain pens. Before that, however, I practiced the art of calligraphy mainly to help with my awful handwriting.

See what I mean? Nice ink though (KWZ EI Dorado if you were curious).

I handwrite drafts for my novel and found calligraphy was helping to make my work actually legible and soon enough my wife, Annika, bought her own. Naturally I had to try it and found she had even better taste than me so I made an executive decision to buy another pen. And then another one and another one until I finished my first complete draft, whereupon, I decided that I had earnt another pen. I bought myself a Lamy 2000 as my reward.

A small selection of my ever-growing collection.

In my epic search for different pens and filling methods, I came across some custom fountain pen websites (Scriptorium Pens, Newton Pens and Edison Pens - definitely check them out) and was inspired to buy yet another pen. A custom one. But alas, after many hours of fruitless searching, I found nothing within my price-range. Exhausted and utterly degected, I mornfully looked over a fountain pen subreddit and discovered that there was lots of advice on making pens. I had access to an engineering lathe and figured I would give it a go - completely kitless.

This is a mess.
